Our History & Coffee Origins

Digital Espresso was born from a simple idea: bring world-class, ethically sourced coffee to the Filipino market. Here are some key terms that define who we are and what we serve.

Arabica
A species of coffee plant known for its smooth, complex flavor profile with notes of fruit, sugar, and berries. Arabica beans account for roughly 60% of global coffee production and thrive at higher altitudes. All of our single-origin offerings are 100% Arabica, sourced from Philippine highland farms.
Robusta
A hardier coffee species with a stronger, more bitter taste and nearly double the caffeine content of Arabica. Robusta is commonly used in espresso blends to add body and a rich crema. We use Robusta from Cavite in our signature house espresso blend.
Fair Trade
A certification and trading partnership that seeks better conditions and fairer terms for coffee farmers. Fair Trade ensures that producers receive a minimum price that covers the cost of sustainable production. Digital Espresso is a proud Fair Trade partner, guaranteeing that every bean we buy supports the livelihoods of local farming communities.

The Team

  2. Barista — Crafts every drink with precision, maintains quality standards, and connects with customers.
  3. Intern — Learns the craft from the ground up, assists with front-of-house tasks, and supports the team.
